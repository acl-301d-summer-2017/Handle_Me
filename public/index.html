    About Handle Me

    Have you come to a creative roadblock while trying to think of a username? Are all of the good ones taken?

    Team KRaMM designed this website with you in mind! Answer the survey questions to generate a personal username! Handle Me has many features for making the username personal. The most useful ones being the save and drag features. Click on a slot once to keep it in the word lineup while continuing to generate new words around it. Change your mind? No problem, simply click on it again to unsave it. The dragging option allows you to chose the way the words are organized and displayed on the screen. Another personalization feature we have would be the way the username is concatenated. You have choices from Snake_Case, camelCase, and -Hyphenation-. Handle Me also has the unique feature of showing you the availability of the username on social media sites. We check Instagram, Twitter, and Github for you! That's right, you don't even have to leave our site to know if it's already been used! How Cool!! Click HERE to return to the homepage! Now, get started and remember to have fun!
